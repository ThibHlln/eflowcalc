eflowcalc.ra3¶

eflowcalc.ra3(flows, datetimes, hydro_years, drainage_area)¶

Average fall rate.

Calculation Details

Identify the flow falls (i.e. the decreases in daily flows from one day to the next). Calculate the mean of these falls.
